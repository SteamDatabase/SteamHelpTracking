[h1]Apple Steam Link App[/h1]

The Steam Link app for iOS extends Steam Link functionality to many iOS phones, tablets, and Apple TVs. Head to [url=https://www.apple.com/app-store/]App Store[/url] to download the free app. The App uses the same streaming technology as Valve's Steam Link, allowing you to play your favorite games and even spectate VR games right from your TV.

[expand type=title title="Frequently Asked Questions"]

[h3]How can I make the text on the screen bigger?[/h3]
The app supports pinch gestures to change the zoom level. Alternatively, you can adjust the Big Picture Mode resolution to a smaller size to improve legibility.

[h3]The game is choppy and unresponsive. How can I improve the experience?[/h3]
The majority of people have found that switching to a 5GHz network significantly improves the gaming experience.

Also, you can switch to the ‘Fast’ streaming quality option from the Streaming settings in the Steam Link.

[h3]Do I need a controller to use the Steam Link app?[/h3]
You can use the Steam Link app without a controller using your touchscreen, however input will be limited with some games.

[h3]Can I use the Steam Link app with my cellular network?[/h3]
Using Remote Play Anywhere with your cellular network may work, depending on the policies of your cellular provider.
