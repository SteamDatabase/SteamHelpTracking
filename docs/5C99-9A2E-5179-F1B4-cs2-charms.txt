[section id=whatare]

[h2]What are Charms?[/h2]
Charms are keychain-like items that can be attached to any weapon in Counter-Strike 2 (except knives). Charms can be detached and re-attached almost anywhere on a weapon.
[/section]

[section id=receive]

[h2]Where do I get Charms from?[/h2]
Charms can be redeemed in-game by spending Armory Credits or purchased through the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/market/search?appid=730&q=charm]Steam Community Market[/url].
[/section]

[section id=detach]

[h2]How do I detach a Charm?[/h2]
To detach a Charm you must first have a Charm Detachment in your Inventory.

Select the weapon with a Charm in your in-game Inventory, then click "Detach Charm" and confirm at the next screen. The Charm will be removed from your weapon and returned to your in-game Inventory.
[/section]

[section id=detachpack]

[h2]Where do I get Charm Detachments?[/h2]
There are three ways to get Charm Detachments:

[list]

[*]Redeem a Charm from the Armory. Each redeemed charm from the Armory comes with 3 Charm Detachments. [/*]
[*]Redeem Charm Detachments from Weekly Care Packages. [/*]
[*]Purchase Charm Detachments in-game. [/*]

[/list]

[/section]

[section id=detachcount]

[h2]Where can I see how many Charm Detachments I have?[/h2]
To see how many Charm Detachments you have, search for "Charm Detachment" in your in-game Inventory.
[/section]
